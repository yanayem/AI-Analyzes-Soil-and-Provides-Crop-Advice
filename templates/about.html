About Salinity Sentinel

A smart soil monitoring system designed to support farmers in salinity-affected coastal regions of Bangladesh.

Our Mission

Soil salinity continues to threaten agriculture in Bangladesh. Our goal is to build a reliable, affordable, and easy-to-use soil monitoring system that empowers farmers with real-time data.

Key Features

Soil Data Analysis

Analyzes soil parameters and recommends the most suitable crops.

Large DataBase

Maintains a rich dataset to improve crop prediction accuracy.

User-Friendly Dashboard

Provides a clean interface for farmers to monitor soil conditions easily.
